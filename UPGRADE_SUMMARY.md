### NEW: AI Ethics Framework
**File:** `ai-ethics-framework.js`

**Components:**
1. **Ethical Decision Matrix** - 7 principles evaluation
2. **Bias Detection** - Identifies & mitigates biases
3. **Privacy Auditor** - Ensures data protection
4. **Transparency Engine** - Generates explanations
5. **Accountability Logger** - Complete audit trail

**Ethical Principles:**
- Beneficence (do good)
- Non-Maleficence (avoid harm)
- Autonomy (respect choice)
- Justice (fair treatment)
- Transparency (explainable)
- Privacy (data protection)
- Accountability (responsibility)

**Red Lines (Never Cross):**
1. Never harm user
2. Never deceive
3. Never violate privacy
4. Never discriminate
5. Never make irreversible changes without consent
6. Never hide AI decisions
7. Never prioritize efficiency over safety
