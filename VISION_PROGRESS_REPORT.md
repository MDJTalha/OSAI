### Major Wins

1. ✅ **Vision 2030 Created** - Complete strategic roadmap

3. ✅ **10 Languages** - Phase 1 i18n complete
4. ✅ **Autonomous Learning** - Self-improving AI

### Lessons Learned

1. **Camera permissions** require user interaction first
2. **Vercel deployment** needs HTTPS for camera
3. **Button binding** must happen after DOM load
4. **Accessibility** should be built-in, not bolted on
5. **Privacy** is a competitive advantage
